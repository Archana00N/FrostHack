Project

There is a earnest problem of availibility of oxygen for patients that are severely affected due to the virus.
With the availability of healthcare facilities decreasing drastically, the struggle to find oxygen is getting worse.



In our country, there is a shortage of cryogenic tankers, i.e. the infamous "Pill shaped liquid oxygen carrying trucks".
It carries, stores and transports liquid gases at sub-zero temperatures. Manufacturing of such vessels requires special technical know how and sophisticated fabrication techniques.
Hence, our countries has also seen a few mishaps regarding those tankers.

With the availibily of healthcare facilities decreasing drastically, the struggle to find oxygen is getting worse.
Our project aims at providing a Statewise list of all Oxygen Companies, Contact People and their contacts for a set group of cities. One can try to contact one or all the nearest cities' oxygen companies in case of any emergency.
